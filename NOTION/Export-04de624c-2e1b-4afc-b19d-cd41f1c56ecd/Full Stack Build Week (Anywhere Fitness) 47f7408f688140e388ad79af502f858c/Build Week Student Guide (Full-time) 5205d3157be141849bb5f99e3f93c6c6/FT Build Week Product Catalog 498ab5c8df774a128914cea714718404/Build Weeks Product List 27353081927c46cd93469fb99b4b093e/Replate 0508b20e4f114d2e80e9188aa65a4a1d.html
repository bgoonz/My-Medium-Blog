Many businesses have leftover food at the end of the day. Replate is an easy to use app that lets them effortlessly have those extras picked up by a Replate volunteer and donated to one of our worthy partner organizations. Choose a convenient time and a volunteer will show up and take your extras to a predetermined place of need.

‚úÖ MVP

1. User can signup/create an account as either a volunteer or a business by providing a unique username, a password and a valid phoneNumber.

2. Authenticated business can create, read, update and delete a businessProfile. At a minimum, this must include the following properties:

username: String

businessName: String

businessAddress: String

phoneNumber: Integer

3. An authenticated business can create a pickupRequest that must include the following properties:

type of food item: String

amount of food item by count / weight: Integer

preferredPickupTime: datetime

4. Authenticated business can cancel (delete) or update a pickupRequest.

5. Authenticated volunteer can create, read, update and delete a volunteerProfile. At a minimum, this must have the following properties:

username: String

volunteerName: String

phoneNumber: Integer

6. Authenticated volunteer can "cancel" (delete) an assignedPickup and it will be removed from the assignedPickup list and added back to the pendingPickup list.

7. Authenticated volunteer can mark an assignedPickup as completed: Bool once the assignment is finished.
