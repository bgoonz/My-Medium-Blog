Use your lambdastudents.com email, even if you have an existing account with another email.

If it's your first time using CodeSignal with your lambdastudents.com email:

  • Follow the link you receive in your @lambdastudents.com email. Note: Be sure to check the Notifications tab in Zoho mail.
  • Complete the fields & click "Quick Sign Up." Be sure to use your @lambdastudents email address! DO NOT USE ANY OF THE "SIGN UP WITH..." BUTTONS.
  • Please be careful to enter your @lambdastudents.com email correctly - typos or an incorrect domain here is one of the most common reasons for not receiving results.
  • You can continue forward and take the test without activating your account or verifying your e-mail address, but make sure you signed up with your @lambdastudents.com email address.
  • If you click the link in your email and it takes you directly to this page but you are not sure if you are logged in with your @lambdastudents.com email, click the "View Sample Test" button.

  • Check your profile to see what email is associated with the account. If it's not your @lambdastudents.com email, either edit your profile to make your @lambdastudents.com email your primary in CodeSignal or sign out.
    • If you sign out, click the assessment link in your email again, and either sign-up with your @lambdastudents.com email (instructions above) or sign-in with your @lambdastudents.com email (instructions below).

  • If you are signed in with your @lambdastudents.com email, click "Back To Test" and then start the test.

After taking your first test:

  • After taking the test, check your email to verify your account for future use. You will have received an email with your temporary username and password. Follow the link to CodeSignal and log in.

  • When you log in, you can edit your user name and password. Be sure to click "Save Changes." You are strongly encouraged to save your username and password in your password manager.

If you've used CodeSignal with your lambdastudents.com email before:

  • Follow the link you receive in your @lambdastudents.com email. Note: Be sure to check the Notifications tab in Zoho mail.
    • Click "Sign In" on the bottom of this screen and sign in using the @lambdastudents.com credentials you previously created.

  • If you click the link in your email and it takes you directly to this page but you are not sure if you are logged in with your @lambdastudents.com email, click the "View Sample Test" button.

Troubleshooting

I didn't get an email with my results:

It can take a couple hours to receive score reports at times, so if you just completed the test and don't have your score report yet, don't worry. If it's been some time and you still don't have it:

  • Email score reports will ONLY be sent to @lambdastudents.com email addresses.
    • If you are sure you signed in with that address, check the Notifications tab in Zoho email. Most email from CodeSignal gets delivered there rather than your inbox.
    • If you're having trouble accessing your @lambdastudents.com email, please see the next section.

Trouble with Okta/Zoho @lambdastudents.com email?

Follow these escalation steps:

  1. Start with this document. It will help you solve many common issues.

2. If you're still having trouble, open a /frontdesk ticket.

3. If you're still having trouble, contact @Julia Richert on Slack.
